About Us

Travio India Pvt. Ltd. operates Travio, a premier booking marketplace connecting travelers with independent hotel partners across India.

Our Mission

We exist to simplify the hotel discovery and booking process. By partnering directly with verified accommodations, we ensure that you find comfortable, safe, and reliable places to stay at the best possible prices.

How We Work

Travio is a platform bridging the gap between travelers and independent hoteliers. We aggregate listings, verify the basic amenities and locations, and facilitate the booking process via direct WhatsApp communication to ensure a personal touch and prompt service.

Please note: Travio acts as an intermediary marketplace and is not the official property owner or operator of the hotels listed on our platform.
